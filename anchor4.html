HISTORY OF COMPUTERS

THIRD GENERATION COMPUTERS(1964 to 1971)

  • Although transistors were great deal of improvement over the vacuum tubes, they generated heat and damaged the sensitive areas of the computer.
  • The Intergreated Circuit(IC) was invented in 1958 by Jack Kilby. It combined electronic components onto a small silicon disc, made from quartz.
  • More advancement made possible the fitings of even more components on a small chip or a semi conductor.
  • Also in third generation computers, the operating systems allowed the machines to run many different applications.
  • These applications were monitored and coordinated by the computer's memory.
